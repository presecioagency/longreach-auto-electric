What We Do

OUR SERVICES

Full-service auto electrical specialists. If it runs on current, we can fix it — from your daily driver to your fully-kitted outback 4WD.

Batteries & Charging
Battery testing, replacement and dual-battery systems for 4WDs and caravans. Anderson plug installations and trailer wiring.
Most Popular
Car Audio & Stereo
Head unit upgrades, speaker installs, amplifiers, subwoofers and full stereo system design for any vehicle.
Driving Lights
LED light bars, spotlights, bull bar lighting and wiring. See everything clearly on those long outback nights.
Air Conditioning
Vehicle A/C servicing, re-gassing and electrical fault diagnosis. Stay cool no matter how hot it gets out west.
Essential for QLD
Solar & Caravan
Solar panel installation and wiring, caravan electrical systems, power leads and 12V setups for life on the road.
Auto Electrical Faults
Complete fault diagnosis and repair. Wiring, alternators, starter motors and everything electrical in your vehicle.
